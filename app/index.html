About Us

ThoughtWorks Arts incubates artists and technologist collaborations to investigate the impacts of emerging technologies on industry, culture and society.

Together, the ThoughtWorks Arts suite of programs is structured to enable practice-based research, directed by cutting-edge artistic inquiry and driven by the capabilities of the global commercial technology community.
