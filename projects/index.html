Projects

Since the program's inception in 2016, residents at ThoughtWorks have been supported in projects which explore emerging technologies, and how they relate to society. Artists have the opportunity to assemble project teams, and often collaborate with ThoughtWorks employees as part of their process.

Heather Dewey-Hagborg: Suppressed Images

A graphic short story that documents a collaboration between Chelsea Manning and Heather Dewey-Hagborg on the artwork Radical Love.

View project

Neil Harbisson: Time Sense

A wearable sensory headband which allows the wearer to feel the passing of the 24-hour clock around the circumference of the head.

View project

Moon Ribas: Seismic Sense

A wearable sensory organ which allows the wearer to feel earthquakes and other seismic activity occurring anywhere in the world.
